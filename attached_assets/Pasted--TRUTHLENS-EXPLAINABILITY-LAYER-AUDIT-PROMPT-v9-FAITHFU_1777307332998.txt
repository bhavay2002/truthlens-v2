•	truncation misalignment ❌

4. ⚖️ EXPLANATION SCALE NORMALIZATION
MUST VERIFY:
•	SHAP, gradients, attention on comparable scale

•	inconsistent scaling across explainers ❌

5. ⚡ PERFORMANCE (TOP PRIORITY)
